About Campus Smart Solutions

Campus Smart Solutions was created to help UW-Oshkosh become more efficient while giving students real engineering and IT experience. The business focuses on smart campus projects and hands-on prototyping help.

Our goal is to connect student ideas with real technology. We help build prototypes, design sensor systems, and support projects that could improve campus life and reduce costs long-term.

Why We Were Created

UW-Oshkosh students and departments need affordable help designing useful technology projects, from smart sensors to class prototypes.

Our Goal

We want to support innovation, entrepreneurship, and real-world learning by helping students turn ideas into working designs.
