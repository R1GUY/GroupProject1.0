Campus Smart Solutions is a student-run business idea that combines smart campus technology with a project prototyping lab. We help departments and engineering students design, test, and build useful systems like parking sensors, classroom occupancy trackers, energy-saving automation, 3D printed parts, Arduino projects, Raspberry Pi builds, CAD designs, and PCB project support.

Why Choose Us?

📡 Smart Sensors

We design sensor-based systems for parking, classrooms, and campus equipment.

⚙️ Automation

We create simple automation ideas that save energy, time, and money around campus.

🧰 Prototyping Lab

Students can get help turning class projects and senior design ideas into real prototypes.

Engineering and IT students gain hands-on experience while helping UW-Oshkosh.
